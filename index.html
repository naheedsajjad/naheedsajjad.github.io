Python-Based Machine Learning Model for Heart Attack Risk

Analyzing Health and Lifestyle Factors Using the Heart 2022 Dataset.

This project analyzes the Heart 2022 dataset to explore lifestyle, demographic, and health factors associated with heart attack occurrences. A Random Forest model was developed to predict heart-attack risk, followed by SMOTE oversampling to address severe class imbalance. The results compare model performance before and after SMOTE, highlighting how balanced data improves recall for minority cases.
